"Less stressful as advertised... best decision I made"






Back-end (trainee)

In my humble opinion, I belive back-end is much difficult than front-end. With that mindset, I prepared for all sorts of frustration I would encounter.

I joined the company a month later than Leon. Akira and Leon would ensure me time and again that the workplace is not as worrying as we imagine it to be but I thought they were just trying to comfort me.

They were tellng the truth! Our managers have a way of engaging us into challenges without applying pressure. What I like the most is the learning process. The training we are provided are well structured and are broken down to steps for better understanding.

Hotbeans emphasizes on meeting deadline as it should. However, to this I fail to analyse how the company is able to held extra-curricular activities with multiple projects on board.

I guess the time we spend on our project is purely productive and we are meeting our targets without even realizing. Without hesitation, I'd recommend working here as it has been the best decision I ever made.
