Leon Saint

"Supportive management... absolutely recommend working here"






Front-end (trainee)

I started my career at Hotbeans on December 2022. In only five months, I have learned more than what I could from textbooks and online tutorials.

The thing that I like most about this company is the supportive management. They make sure that there are seniors around trainees at all times so that our questions are answered immediately. Sometimes we are left by ourselves if the project is not highly complicated but I understand that it is for our own benefit as we should be able to tackle the challenges by ourselves.

The skills that I have learned in short amount of time are basic programming knowledges and how to implement them in websites. Most online tutorials only teach us the definition and purpose of coding lines but do not give examples how to apply them. Other than that, I have learned to work in a team as tasks are divided among the members and being able to meet the deadline is somewhat satisfying.

I would absolutely recommend working in Hotbeans to my friends if they ever show interest in changing their career to IT and Computing.
