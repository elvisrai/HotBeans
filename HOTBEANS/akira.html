Akira Sato

"So much fun... inspiring and motivating"






Full-stack (trainee)

I was selected by Hotbeans to work here for my enthusiastic personality and willingess to learn more. Being a full-stack trainee, I always had a misconception that my job will be more stressful than the rest. But I was wrong to think so.

When I first joined in August 2022, I was really nervous about the job. I thought I would be pressured to meet the expectations and deadlines. It is still surprising how the managers and every other team members manage to make projects interesting.

Yes we divide our tasks and challenges are different each time but everyone is extremely friend and the seniors often check how I am doing or if I need help with anything.

The best thing that I like about working here is the opportunity to grow and learn. We conduct brief meetings at the end of the day and ask what challenges we faced today, how we approached it and how we can do better.

Honestly, this experience has been so much inspiring and motivating for me. I geniuenly look up to my seniors' work ethic.
